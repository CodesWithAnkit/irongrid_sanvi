Frequently asked

Quick answers for guests evaluating Sanvi

Is Sanvi multi-tenant?

Yes — organizations (tenants) are isolated by default. You can onboard multiple tenants with per-org branding and roles.

Can I import data from CSV?

Yes — customers and products can be bulk imported with validation and duplicate detection.

Do you support custom integrations?

We provide APIs, webhooks, and an integration team for ERP and accounting systems (Enterprise plan).
